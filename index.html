Welcome to coaching for a realistic french accent

My name is Claire Chateau, a french accent coach for actors, singers and public speakers.

Originally from Bordeaux, a wine-making region in the South West of France, I moved to Paris to study linguistics, languages and Teaching F.L.E (French as a Foreign Language.)

I am hard-working and always ensure my students receive the very best pedagogy. I’m patient and dedicated. I’ll teach you the skills needed to acquire an authentic and convincing French accent. We’ll work together at getting your pronunciation and mannerism right, so you can act out French with complete panache.


Coaching You

I’ll design a programme adapted to your needs, which matches your learning style. Together we’ll work at creating the voice and speech of your character, the song you are learning or simply ensuring you ‘fit’ within a French setting

We’ll look at your voice, body language, facial expressions and other culturally encoded aspects of the French identity. We’ll also look at syntax and grammar to polish your French accent.
