How my coaching will work for you...

I’ll design a programme adapted to your needs, which matches your learning style. Together we’ll work at creating the voice and speech of your character, the song you are learning or simply ensuring you ‘fit’ within a French setting.

We’ll look at your voice, body language, facial expressions and other culturally encoded aspects of the French identity. We’ll also look at syntax and grammar to polish your French accent.

We’ll have fun, practical and goal-orientated sessions and the French you will learn will help you succeed in your personal or professional life.

I can be available for on-set sessions and training can also be given on skype if you are not in Auckland, New Zealand.


My methods will suit...

Actors who want to create a solid French accent so they can expand their portfolio or prepare for an upcoming role

Business professionals, or public speakers expanding their market to French speaking places

Students auditioning or doing voice-over for commercials or singers entering competitions
